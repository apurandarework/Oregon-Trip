Explore Oregon

Time of day
Distance from Airbnb
Garden 🌅 Morning / Afternoon

International Rose Test Garden

Over 10,000 rose bushes with sweeping Mt. Hood views. America's oldest public rose test garden — a perfect pairing with the Japanese Garden nearby. Peak bloom in late May.

🕐Open daily 7:30 AM – 9 PM
💰FREE entry · Free guided tours 1 PM daily (Memorial Day–Labor Day)
📍~11 mi from Airbnb · ~20 min drive · Washington Park
★★★★★ Peak bloom during your visit!
Garden 🌅 Morning best

Portland Japanese Garden

12-acre sanctuary with 8 garden styles, koi ponds, bonsai terrace, and a tea house — acclaimed as the most authentic Japanese garden outside Japan. Allow 1.5–3 hrs.

🕐Wed–Mon 10 AM – 6 PM · Closed Tuesdays
💰Adult $22.50 · Youth (6–17) $16.50 · Under 5 Free
💡Book tickets online in advance — sells out on sunny days
📍~11 mi from Airbnb · ~20 min drive · Washington Park
★★★★★ Arrive at open for fewest crowds
Museum 🔬 All Day

OMSI – Oregon Museum of Science

219,000+ sq ft of hands-on exhibits, a planetarium, the USS Blueback submarine, giant-screen theater, and science labs. The ultimate rainy-day destination. Plan 3–4 hours.

🕐Tue–Fri 9:30 AM–5:30 PM · Sat 9:30 AM–7 PM · Sun 9:30 AM–5:30 PM
💰Adult $22.50 · Youth (3–13) $15.50 · Planetarium/Sub add $7–10
🅿️Parking: $5/2 hrs · $8 all day · $2 after 5 PM
📍~13 mi from Airbnb · ~22 min drive · Central Eastside, Portland
★★★★☆ Great for all ages
Food & Neighborhood 🌙 Afternoon / Evening

Pearl District & Food Carts

Portland's trendiest walkable district — galleries, Powell's Books (largest indie bookstore), boutiques, and world-famous food cart pods. Best explored on foot over 3+ hours.

🕐Food carts open ~11 AM–8 PM · Restaurants 11 AM–10 PM
💰FREE to explore · Food ~$10–20/person
📍~12 mi from Airbnb · ~20 min drive · Downtown Portland
★★★★★ Don't miss Powell's Books
Food Tour ☀️ Afternoon best

Portland Guided Food Tour

Explore Portland's legendary culinary scene — food carts, local restaurants, artisan producers — with a knowledgeable local guide through the city's best neighborhoods.

🕐Tours typically 11 AM–2 PM or 1 PM–4 PM
💰~$75–$110 per person · Book online in advance
📍~12 mi from Airbnb · ~20 min drive · Downtown Portland
★★★★★ Highly recommended
Garden 🌅 Morning / Afternoon

Lan Su Chinese Garden

A tranquil Ming Dynasty–style garden built by artisans from Suzhou, China. Features pavilions, a teahouse, and a serene koi lake in Portland's historic Chinatown. Allow 1–2 hrs.

🕐Daily 10 AM – 5 PM (last entry 4:30 PM)
💰Adult $14 · Senior $11 · Youth (6–18) $9 · Under 6 Free
📍~13 mi from Airbnb · ~22 min drive · Old Town/Chinatown
★★★★★ Peaceful city escape
Experience 🌙 Evening only

Willamette River Dinner Cruise

Cruise the Willamette at sunset with a full dinner, drinks, and panoramic Portland skyline views. Operated by Portland Spirit — a memorable Saturday evening highlight.

🕐Typically 7 PM – 10 PM · Check Portland Spirit for exact times
💰~$85–$120 per person · Reservations required
📍~13 mi from Airbnb · ~20 min drive · Tom McCall Waterfront Park
★★★★☆ Book early — popular on weekends
Wine / Day Trip 🍷 Full Day Trip

Willamette Valley Wine Country

World-famous Pinot Noir across 700+ wineries in rolling Dundee Hills. May is Oregon Wine Month — special tastings, live music, and vineyard events all weekend. Visit 3–4 wineries for a full day.

🕐Most tasting rooms 11 AM – 5 PM · Some require reservations
💰Tasting flights $20–$65/winery · Guided day tours ~$95–$120/person
📍~35 mi from Airbnb 🗺️ Trek · ~45 min via OR-99W through Tigard → Sherwood → Dundee
★★★★★ May = Oregon Wine Month — special events!
